Life Cycles & Growth
4 points — Choose the best answer.
Plant Life Cycle 1 pt
1A bean plant starts as a seed. What is the correct order of its life cycle?
  • ASeed → Flower → Seedling → Mature plant
  • BSeed → Seedling → Mature plant → Flower → New seeds
  • CFlower → Seed → Mature plant → Seedling
  • DMature plant → Seedling → Seed → Flower
Animal Life Cycle 1 pt
2A butterfly goes through four stages: egg, larva (caterpillar), pupa (chrysalis), and adult butterfly. What makes this life cycle different from a dog's life cycle?
  • AThe butterfly stays the same shape throughout its whole life
  • BThe butterfly changes dramatically in body shape and structure as it grows — the dog does not
  • CDogs and butterflies have exactly the same life cycle
  • DThe dog changes shape more dramatically than the butterfly
Inherited Traits 1 pt
3A puppy has brown fur, just like both of its parents. What kind of trait is this?
  • AAn acquired trait — the puppy learned it from watching its parents
  • BAn inherited trait — the puppy received it from its parents through reproduction
  • CA survival adaptation — the puppy chose brown fur to blend in
  • DA random trait — it has nothing to do with the parents
Variation 1 pt
4Five kittens are born to the same two cats. They all have different fur patterns. Which statement best explains this?
  • AThe kittens are not related because their traits are different
  • BOffspring inherit traits from both parents but are not identical to either parent or to each other
  • COnly one kitten inherited its traits — the others got their traits from somewhere else
  • DTraits in animals are always identical from parent to offspring

Traits & Evidence
4 points — Use what you learned to explain.
Plant Needs & Growth 1 pt
5A class grows two identical bean plants. Plant A gets sunlight and regular water. Plant B is kept in a dark closet with no water. After two weeks, Plant A is tall and green. Plant B is yellow and wilted. What do the results show about what plants need? Use the data as evidence.
💡 Start with: "The results show that plants need ___. The evidence is that Plant A ___ while Plant B ___."
Compare Life Cycles 1 pt
6A frog goes through four stages: egg → tadpole → froglet → adult frog. A human goes through: baby → child → teenager → adult. Name one way these life cycles are similar and one way they are different.
💡 Start with: "Both a frog and a human ___. One difference is that the frog ___ while the human ___."
Trait Data 1 pt
7A class surveys 20 students for one trait: attached vs. detached earlobes. Results: 13 detached, 7 attached. What does this data tell you about inherited traits in this group? What pattern do you notice?
💡 Start with: "I notice that ___. This tells me that inherited traits ___."
Survival Advantage 1 pt
8In a herd of deer, most have brown fur. A few are born with lighter tan fur. A wildfire burns the grasses in the area, leaving light-colored dry fields. Which deer might be harder for predators to spot? Explain why this trait difference matters.
💡 Start with: "The deer with ___ fur might be harder to spot because ___. This trait matters because ___."
